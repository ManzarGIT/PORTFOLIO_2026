Spotify Dashboard Analysis

Exploring Spotify streaming data to reveal artist performance and listener trends.

1. Raw Data & Challenge

Massive Spotify dataset with millions of tracks containing artist, genre, and audio features. Cleaned and structured for analysis in Power BI.

2. Cleaning & Preparation

  • Removed duplicates and fixed artist name inconsistencies.
  • Handled missing audio features and standardized release years.
  • Created calculated columns for average popularity and streams.

3. Insights

  • Measured top artists and songs by total streams.
  • Identified popular genres and danceability trends.
  • Tracked KPIs like total streams and unique artists.

4. Outcome

Delivered an interactive Power BI dashboard revealing music trends and artist growth patterns over time.
