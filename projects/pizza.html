Pizza Sales Analysis

Analyzing pizza sales using SQL for business insights.

1. Challenge & Data

Four CSVs—orders, order_details, pizzas, pizza_types—were combined into a relational dataset to analyze revenue, size popularity, and top pizza types.

2. Cleaning & Preparation

  • Standardized pizza names and categories.
  • Fixed dates and handled missing entries.
  • Joined tables via primary/foreign keys.

3. Key Insights

  • Evening hours = peak orders.
  • Large pizzas = higher revenue share.
  • Top 5 pizza types drove 40% of sales.

4. Outcome

SQL provided clear business answers, forming a base for Power BI dashboards tracking pizza demand, size mix, and revenue growth.
