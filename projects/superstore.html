Superstore Sales Analysis

Showcasing the journey from raw data to a Power BI dashboard.

1. Goal

The goal was to analyze Superstore sales data and uncover insights to support business decisions. Data included orders, customers, products, regions, and discounts.

2. Data Cleaning & Preparation

Cleaned in Power Query / Power BI: removed duplicates, fixed nulls, standardized regions, and created calculated fields like profit margins and discount impact.

3. Insights

Technology led sales; office supplies yielded higher profit margins. The American region performed best while Asia showed growth potential.

4. Final Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard offered KPIs, category drill-downs, and regional maps—enabling fast, data-driven decisions.
